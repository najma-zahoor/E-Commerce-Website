Our Story

From humble beginnings to a trusted e-commerce platform

2010

The Beginning

ShopCart started as a small online store with just 50 products. Our founder, Sarah Johnson, wanted to create a shopping experience that was simple, reliable, and customer-focused.

2014

Expansion

We expanded our product range to over 1,000 items and introduced our first mobile app, making shopping more accessible than ever before.

2018

Innovation

Implemented AI-powered recommendations and launched our premium membership program, offering exclusive benefits to our loyal customers.

2024

Today

Now serving customers nationwide with over 10,000 products, same-day delivery in major cities, and an award-winning customer service team.
