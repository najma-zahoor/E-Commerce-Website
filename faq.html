Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary based on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Next Day Delivery: 1 business day (order before 2 PM)

International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the United States.

For international orders, free shipping is available on orders over $100.

During promotional periods, we may offer free shipping on all orders regardless of the total amount.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to:

  • Track your order on our website in your account section
  • Track directly with the carrier (UPS, FedEx, or USPS)
  • Use our mobile app for real-time updates

If you haven't received a tracking number within 2 business days, please contact our support team.

What countries do you ship to?

We currently ship to over 50 countries worldwide including:

  • United States
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Australia
  • European Union countries
  • Japan
  • South Korea
  • And many more...

During checkout, you can see if we ship to your country. Some restrictions may apply to certain products.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. To be eligible for a return:

  • Item must be in original condition
  • All tags and packaging must be intact
  • Proof of purchase is required

Note: Some items are non-returnable including:

  • Personalized/customized products
  • Downloadable software
  • Gift cards
  • Perishable goods

How do I return an item?

Follow these simple steps to return an item:

  1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  2. Select the item you want to return
  3. Choose a return reason and follow the instructions
  4. Print the return label provided
  5. Package the item securely with the return label
  6. Drop off at any authorized carrier location

Once we receive your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

How long do refunds take?

Refund processing times vary based on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Apple Pay/Google Pay: 3-7 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-14 business days

The refund timeline starts from when we receive your returned item at our warehouse.

Note: Your bank may take additional time to post the refund to your account.

Orders & Payment

How can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it:

  • To cancel: Go to your order history and click "Cancel Order"
  • To modify: Cancel the order and place a new one with correct details

Important: Once your order has been processed for shipping, it cannot be canceled or modified.

If you need to change the shipping address after the 1-hour window, please contact our support team immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a wide variety of payment methods:

  • All major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • Debit cards
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • ShopCart Gift Cards
  • ShopPay (our payment plan option)

All payments are processed securely using SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We take security very seriously:

  • SSL encryption on all pages
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • No sensitive payment data is stored on our servers
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Two-factor authentication available for accounts

We use industry-leading payment processors to ensure your information is protected. You can also enable additional security features in your account settings.

Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for the password reset link
  5. Click the link and create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Make sure you're checking the correct email address
  • Try resending the password reset email
  • Contact support if you still have issues

How do I update my account information?

To update your account information:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Click on the section you want to update:
    • Personal Info: Name, email, phone number
    • Password: Change your password
    • Addresses: Shipping and billing addresses
    • Preferences: Email notifications, language, currency
  4. Make your changes and click "Save"

Note: Changing your email address requires verification through the new email.

Products & Services

What is your warranty policy?

Our warranty policy varies by product category:

  • Electronics: 1-year manufacturer warranty
  • Home Appliances: 2-year warranty
  • Clothing: No warranty, but 30-day return policy
  • Accessories: 6-month warranty

Warranty coverage includes manufacturing defects but does not cover:

  • Accidental damage
  • Misuse or improper handling
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications

For warranty claims, please contact our support team with your order number and details of the issue.

Do you offer product installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for select products:

  • Home Electronics: TV mounting, sound system setup
  • Major Appliances: Refrigerator, washer, dryer installation
  • Furniture: Assembly services
  • Smart Home Devices: Installation and setup

Installation services are available during checkout for eligible products. Pricing varies based on the product and complexity of installation.

Our certified technicians will schedule an appointment at your convenience and ensure everything is installed correctly.
